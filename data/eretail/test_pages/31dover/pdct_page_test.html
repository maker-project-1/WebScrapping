OVERVIEW

Dynastie features the full range of Armand de Brignac bottles from the regular-sized 750ml right up to the 30 litre Midas bottle.

Armand de Brignac’s global director of PR and brand communications, Yvonne Lardner, said: “Dynastie is an unparalleled concept in the Champagne industry. We’re only able to make very few large formats, especially our 30L Midas, so production of the Armand de Brignac Dynastie will be highly limited.”

As well as the two extremes mentioned above, the bottles within the Dynastie collection include a 1.5l Magnum, 3l Jeroboam, 4.5l Rehoboam, 6l Methuselah, 9l Salmanazar, 12l Balthazar and 15l Nebuchadnezzar. The huge 30l Midas bottle is equivalent to 40 standard bottles of Champagne.
